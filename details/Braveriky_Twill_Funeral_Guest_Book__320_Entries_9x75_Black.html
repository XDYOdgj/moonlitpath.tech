Braveriky Twill Funeral Guest Book

More than just a keepsake, this guest book is a heartfelt tribute to your loved one. Designed for beauty and function, it's perfect for any memorial service, funeral, or event where you want to preserve heartfelt messages.

Timeless Elegance

Featuring gold foil lettering on the cover and a refined twill fabric exterior, this guest book has a classic appeal that complements any setting. Its eye-catching design makes it a memorable piece for guests and a meaningful keepsake for you.

Thoughtfully Designed

Each page is crafted with care, using 48 sheets of thick, durable 120 gsm paper that ensures entries remain clear and legible. The built-in memory table sign and sign-in card offer a complete solution for organizing guest sign-ins and creating a personal touch during your event.

Easy to Use

With a sewn binding, the pages lay flat, making it simple and comfortable for guests to write their messages. This allows for a seamless experience whether you're hosting a private gathering or a larger event.

Versatile & Functional

This guest book is not only for funerals but also works well for weddings, birthdays, or any meaningful occasion. It even includes a dedicated section for recording donations and sending personal thank you notes, adding an extra layer of appreciation.

  • High-quality hardcover with faux twill fabric and gold foil lettering
  • Includes memory table sign and sign-in card for quick setup
  • 48 sheets of 120 gsm bleed-proof paper for durability
